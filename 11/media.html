About Seedhe Maut

A grim track with an eerie production, ‘Taakat’ addresses the sinister shackles of the glorified rat race embedded in India’s education system which continues into the hunt for employment. Seedhe Maut revokes every expectation that society attached to them as young men, finding solace only in their music. Featuring Lil Bhavi and produced by DJ SA and Calm, the track Taakat is relentless in its pursuit to shatter every social convention imposed on them since childhood.
